About Us

Discover a culinary haven at Qrispy, where passion and flavor converge in the heart of Delhi. Established in 1989, our chef, Mr. Kamal Qureshi, transforms locally sourced, seasonal ingredients into culinary masterpieces that redefine dining. At Qrispy, we prioritize sustainability, weaving eco-friendly practices into our operations. Our commitment extends to the community—we actively support local initiatives, making us more than just a restaurant. Experience warmth and hospitality in every visit, whether it's a special occasion or a casual gathering with friends. Join us at Qrispy for a dining adventure that transcends expectations. Thank you for choosing us—we can't wait to craft memorable moments with you. Qrispy - Where Flavor Meets Heart in Every Bite.
